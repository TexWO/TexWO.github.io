I am a Software Engineer and current MSc student in Computer Science at the [IT University of Copenhagen](https://www.itu.dk) (ITU), building on a solid foundation from my Bachelor’s degree at [Aalborg University](https://www.aau.dk) (AAU).

I am passionate about systems programming, clean architecture, and the intersection of theoretical computer science and practical application. Throughout my studies and personal projects, I have developed a familiarity with a broad stack of programming languages, including C, C++, OCaml, TypeScript, and Java.

My approach to engineering is language-agnostic; I focus on understanding underlying computational principles to solve complex problems efficiently. My portfolio showcases a variety of projects—ranging from low-level systems implementations to robust web applications that demonstrate my ability to adapt to new paradigms and deliver high-quality code.

I am always interested in discussing new technologies, open-source contributions, or collaborative research.
